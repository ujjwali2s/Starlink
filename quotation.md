# PROPOSAL & QUOTATION
**Corporate Website Development**

**Date:** January 28, 2026
**To:** Starlink World Qatar
**Project:** Corporate Website Design & Development

---

## 1. Executive Summary
We are pleased to submit this proposal for the design and development of the new Starlink World corporate website. Our goal is to create a premium, responsive, and content-rich digital presence that reflects Starlink's status as a leader in Qatar's technology and retail sectors.

## 2. Scope of Work (Sitemap)

The website will consist of the following structured sections and functionalities:

### **1. Home**
- Dynamic Hero Section (Video/Slider)
- High-level overview of services
- "Our Business" Highlights
- Trusted Partners Showcase
- Quick CTA access

### **2. About Us**
- **Overview:** Company introduction and mission.
- **Our Leadership:** Executive team profiles.
- **Our Board of Directors:** Board member bios.
- **Our Story:** Company history and timeline.
- **Awards & Recognition:** Showcase of achievements.
- **Corporate Social Responsibility (CSR):** Community initiatives.

### **3. Our Business Solutions**
- **Overview:** Summary of all verticals.
- **Consumer Tech:** Retail, E-commerce, and Distribution.
- **ICT:** Managed Services, Infrastructure, and System Integration.
- **Business Process Outsourcing (BPO):** Contact Centers and HR Solutions.

### **4. Careers**
- Job listings integration.
- Culture and benefits section.
- Application form/portal.

### **5. Newsroom**
- **Latest News:** Press releases and updates.
- **Articles & Insights:** Blog/Thought leadership content.

### **6. Contact Us**
- Contact forms.
- Location maps (Retail & Office).
- Department-specific contact info.

## 3. Technical Specifications
- **Framework:** Next.js (React) for high performance and SEO.
- **CMS:** Headless CMS implementation for easy content updates (News, Jobs, Team).
- **Responsive Design:** Mobile-first approach.
- **Security:** SSL integration, secure forms.
- **Analytics:** Google Analytics 4 setup.

## 4. Project Timeline
*Estimated Duration: 4-6 Weeks*
- Week 1: Discovery & UI/UX Design
- Week 2-3: Development & CMS Integration
- Week 4: Content Population
- Week 5: Testing & QA
- Week 6: Deployment & Training
